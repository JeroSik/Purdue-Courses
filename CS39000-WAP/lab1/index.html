AHHPS

Association of Hip Hop Practitioners and Scholars (formerly Purdue Boiler Breakers) is a student organization at Purdue University that promotes Hip Hop culture and its various forms of expression. We spread awareness through performance, and discussion. Join our group to learn more about American popular culture, and opportunities to learn from practitioners and scholars!
